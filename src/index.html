Other Templates

Have a look at these free template sites to find your style and preference

HTML5 UP Website:

What’s Good: High-quality, modern responsive templates. They’re all free under the Creative Commons license. Code is clean, making it relatively easy to customize further.

Start Bootstrap Website:

What’s Good: Free, fully responsive templates built on the Bootstrap framework, making them flexible and easy to work with. A broad range of styles (landing pages, agency pages, portfolios) you can tailor to a professional chef’s branding.

CSS Templates Website:

What’s Good: Large selection of free templates. Templates vary in style, so you’ll likely find something simple, elegant, and suitable for a culinary theme.

One Page Love Website:

What’s Good: Specializes in one-page layouts—ideal for a simple site to start with. Attractive designs that focus on storytelling and visuals (perfect for showcasing dishes and a personal chef’s credentials)

Templated Website:

What’s Good: All templates are responsive and built on HTML5 and CSS3. Minimalistic designs that are good for professional services wanting a clean, modern look.

W3 Schools Website:

What’s Good: Free templates built on the W3.CSS framework. They are simple, clean, and easy to customize. Good for a chef who wants a straightforward, no-frills site.
